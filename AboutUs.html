DISTRITO DE CAPIZ VIRTUAL MUSEUM FOR THE PROVINCE OF CAPIZ

The Distrito De Capiz Virtual Museum for the Province of Capiz is developed for graphical representation of different museum. Providing this system, it could be easy for the user to view and see what the museum looks-like and get some information inside the museum. It will provide a system that enables a viewer to experience a virtual museum in every municipality in Capiz such as the Panublion Museum, Balay ni Nanay Lording (Panay, Capiz), Ivisan Museum, Shell Museum (Pilar, Capiz) and Pontevedra Museum. It will give information and ideas to the user and visitors about the museum.

Museums provide a unique interactive experience of getting up close to things we usually only see in books, newspapers or on the television. This project is designed for Visitors, Students and Personnel of the Museum.
